Lemon Garlic Chicken

Lemon Garlic Chicken served with asparagus

Bone-in chicken thighs combine with white wine,

heavy cream, and garlic for a decadent dinner option.

Create exquisite flavor in under an hour.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • White Wine
  • Heavy Cream
  • Lemon
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare Pan with butter
  2. Add lots of garlic
  3. Add Salt and Pepper to Chicken
  4. Put Chicken in Pan
  5. Add heavy Cream
  6. Add White Wine
